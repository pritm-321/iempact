About Us

IEMPACT, the Annual Cultural Fest of IEM, is back again with its 33rd edition. This year, we hope to celebrate IEMPACT on the theme - UNESCO World Heritage - on the occasion of Shantiniketan being declared a World Heritage site recently by UNESCO. At the Annual Cultural Fest of IEM, Salt Lake, we will bring to you a variety of events and competitions like Eastern and Western Dance, Eastern and Western Music, Quizzard, Debate, Creative Writing, Beatboxing, Futsal (football), Treasure Hunt, Face Painting, Bhuri Bhoj (a food festival) and most importantly hope and joy. We wish to see dazzling smiles on the faces of all the participants and audience members at our event as they encounter a cultural explosion of Folk Fusion like no other. Together, let us dive into a world where ektaras and khols come alive, where the heart dances with the intoxicating and inviting beats of Bihu, where folk is not just a genre, it's a rich tradition, it's an emotion, it's joy ! Together, let us dive into the enchanting world of IEMPACT 2024.

EVENTS

FOOD

Full fill your cravings here. Taste the delicious food from the near by stall.

Eastern Music

If you are the melody monarch of raagas and taans, then we have the perfect competition for you. IEMPACT presents Eastern Music, where you have the chance to let the sunray of your voice reach the dreariest corners and fill them with endless light. Come, join us and let your raagas warm the heart of the audience like spring after a long winter. May the thousand lanterns of years of riyaaz light your way forward!

Western Music

If the sequin of your voice twinkle like the stars of the most magnificent constellation when you sing Western genres, then we have the perfect competition for you. IEMPACT presents Westwood, where you have the chance to be crowned the nightingale of one of the biggest voice-wars in town. Come, join us as your passion and style take the centre stage. May your voice dazzle like diamonds and reach the hearts of many

QUIZZARD

Quiz geeks ahoy! If answers tickle at the tip of your tongue, perhaps even before a question has been asked, then we have the perfect competition for you. IEMPACT presents Quizzard, where the sharpness of the sword of your knowledge will be tested as you fiercely battle against the best quizzers Kolkata has to offer. Come, join us as you pick the grains of sand on the endless shore of knowledge. May the pegasus of your GK take you to the moon!

DEBATE

If you've always wanted to take the stand and be the change, then we have the perfect competition for you. IEMPACT presents Debate, one of the biggest war-of-words in town. Come, join us as your words find wings and reach the highest clouds. May the iron grip of your logic and mind never falter !

FACE PAINTING

You don't need a canvas to create a masterpiece. But if the artist in you is still looking for the perfect canvas, then you don't have to look far. IEMPACT presents Face Art, an event which welcomes you to unleash your creativity by painting your face. So splash the colours fearlessly and have fun !

Beatboxing

If beatboxing is your passion and you are searching for a platform to show off, we have the perfect competition for you. IEMPACT presents VoxBox. Come, join us and grab the spotlight with your beats. Do you think you are the emperor of beats? Then we welcome you to show off your skills at one of the biggest beatboxing competitions in Kolkata.

Creative Writing

If you have a flare for writing and are searching for a platform to show off, then we have the perfect competition for you. IEMPACT presents Creative Writing, where you will get to bring your talent for writing to the forefront. Come, join us as you pour your heart out on a piece of paper!

Step Up

If you think your sparkling dance moves are powerful enough to snatch the spotlight, then we have the perfect competition for you. IEMPACT presents Step Up, where you will get a chance to take the dance floor by storm with your style and charisma. Come, join us and take this opportunity to shine like a star on the horizon of IEMPACT!

Eastern dance

If you carry the flavour of the Eastern culture in your 'nritya', then we have the perfect competition for you to show off your talent. IEMPACT presents Eastern Euphoria, a competition which promises to take the legacy of Eastern Dance forward. Come, join us and enchant the audience with your 'adavus' and 'mudras' as they keep pace with the rhythm of your 'ghunghru'. Keeping your poise and grace as a flurry of movements envelop you and striving to express a part of yourself throughout the process - dance might almost be an extended metaphor of life itself. Be it Bharatnatyam, Kathak, Odissi or any other form, we welcome you to dance like a flame on stage.

Futsal

The goal is to win as many of the first four matches and then go through the knock out stages. The tournament has specific rules. Get ready to score more goals than your opponent team.

Shipwreck

Dive into the thrilling depths of our college fest's Shipwreck event! Brace yourself for a nautical adventure like never before. Participants will navigate stormy seas of challenges, showcasing their resilience and teamwork. From creative problem-solving to quick thinking, this event tests various skills. Join us for an unforgettable voyage into the unknown, where only the strongest will emerge victorious. Anchors aweigh for Shipwreck at our fest!
